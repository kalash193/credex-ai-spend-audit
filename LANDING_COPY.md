## FAQ

### Is this free?

Yes. The audit is free and shows results before asking for an email.

### Do I need to connect billing?

No. The MVP uses manual inputs so teams can get a quick answer without OAuth or finance permissions.

### Is the math AI-generated?

No. The audit math uses deterministic rules and cited pricing. AI is only used for the personalized written summary.

### When does Credex appear?

Credex is surfaced prominently when the audit finds more than $500/month in likely savings.

### What happens to my public report?

The public report strips identifying lead fields and shows only stack, spend, recommendations, and savings.
